Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Leader Operations Center ("LOC") respects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when using LOC.

Information You Enter

The app may allow users to enter leadership-related information such as notes, checklists, readiness statuses, and user-created records. This information is used to provide app functionality and help users organize leadership tasks inside the app.

Local Storage

Some data may be stored locally on the user's device. Users are responsible for managing the information they enter and for following all applicable organizational policies.

Data Sharing

  • LOC does not sell personal information.
  • LOC does not share user data with advertisers.
  • Information is used to provide app functionality.

Future Cloud or Account Features

If cloud sync, account features, shared access, or similar services are added later, this Privacy Policy will be updated to describe how those features handle information.

Restricted Information Warning

Users should not enter classified information, controlled unclassified information, protected health information, or restricted information unless authorized by applicable policy.

Independent Application

LOC is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the U.S. Department of Defense or U.S. Army.

Contact

For privacy questions, support, bug reports, or feature requests, contact leaderoperationscenter@gmail.com.
